About Literacy India Bijwasan Centre

Our Mission

Literacy India Bijwasan Centre is dedicated to empowering underprivileged girls and women in Delhi through quality education, vocational training, and digital literacy programs.

Founded in 1996, Literacy India has touched over 6.1 million lives across 165+ centres in 20 states. The Bijwasan Centre specifically focuses on the Sampurna Shiksha initiative, providing comprehensive educational support to girls from Class 6 to 10.

Location: Near Old Water Tank, Bijwasan, New Delhi

Our Vision

To create self-sufficient communities by empowering underprivileged children, youth, and women through education, skilling, and holistic development.

We believe in the 4Es: Education, Employability, Empowerment, and Environment.

Our approach combines hands-on learning experiences with practical skill development, ensuring that every girl has the opportunity to build a better future for herself and her community.
